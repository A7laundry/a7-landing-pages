FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

How long does carpet cleaning take?+
Most rooms take 20-30 minutes each. A typical 3-bedroom home takes about 1.5-2 hours including setup and inspection. We work efficiently without cutting corners.
How long until carpets are dry?+
Carpets typically dry within 4-6 hours with our speed-dry fan service. We recommend keeping windows open and fans running. In most cases, you can walk on carpets (with clean socks) within 2 hours.
Is it safe for pets and children?+
Absolutely! We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning solutions that are 100% safe for children, pets, and people with allergies. Our products are Green Seal certified.
Do you move furniture?+
Yes, we move basic furniture (chairs, small tables, ottomans) at no extra charge. Heavy items like beds, dressers, and large entertainment centers stay in place ‚Äî we clean around and under them where accessible.
Can you remove tough stains?+
We remove most stains including pet accidents, wine, coffee, and food spills. Some stains (like bleach or permanent dye) may be permanent, but our technicians will assess and be upfront about what to expect.
How often should I have carpets cleaned?+
We recommend professional cleaning every 6-12 months for most homes. If you have pets, kids, or allergies, every 3-6 months is ideal. Regular cleaning extends carpet life by 5-10 years.
What's included in the free room offer?+
Your 1st room is completely free when you book whole-house carpet cleaning (3+ rooms). The free room includes full pre-treatment, hot-water extraction, stain treatment, and deodorizer. Standard room up to 200 sq ft.
What areas do you service?+
We service the entire Orlando metro area including Winter Park, Lake Nona, Dr. Phillips, Windermere, Kissimmee, Celebration, and surrounding communities. Use our ZIP checker below to verify your area.
