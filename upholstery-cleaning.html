FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our upholstery cleaning.

What types of upholstery do you clean?+
We clean all types including cotton, linen, polyester, microfiber, velvet, chenille, and leather. Each fabric type gets a customized cleaning approach for optimal results without damage.
How long does upholstery cleaning take?+
A standard sofa takes 30-45 minutes. A full living room set (sofa + loveseat + chair) typically takes 1.5-2 hours. Drying time is 2-4 hours depending on fabric type.
What is Scotchgard protection?+
Scotchgard is a professional-grade fabric protector by 3M. It creates an invisible barrier that repels liquids and prevents stains from setting. Spills bead up on the surface instead of soaking into the fabric, giving you time to blot them away.
Is it safe for homes with pets?+
Absolutely! Our cleaning solutions are non-toxic and pet-safe. We specialize in removing pet hair, dander, and odors. Pets can return to the furniture once it's dry (2-4 hours).
Can you remove old stains?+
We successfully remove most stains including food, drinks, pet accidents, and body oils. Very old or set-in stains may lighten significantly even if not fully removed. We'll assess honestly before starting.
Do you clean at my home or take furniture away?+
All cleaning is done on-site at your home. Our mobile equipment is professional-grade. No need to move heavy furniture ‚Äî we come to you and clean everything in place.
How often should I have upholstery cleaned?+
We recommend every 12-18 months for most homes. With pets or kids, every 6-12 months. Regular professional cleaning extends furniture life by 5-7 years and maintains a healthy home environment.
What areas do you service?+
We service the entire Orlando metro area including Winter Park, Winter Garden, Lake Mary, Oviedo, Dr. Phillips, Windermere, Kissimmee, and surrounding communities. Use our ZIP checker to verify.
